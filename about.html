Welcome to WurkWurk!

WurkWurk is a simple tool to help you keep a running log of what you're working on. It's designed to be private, simple, and all yours.

How It Works

  1. It's Your Data: WurkWurk sends all logs to a private Google Sheet that you own and control.
  2. Periodic Popups: At an interval you set (e.g., every 15 minutes), a popup will appear asking what you're doing.
  3. Log Your Work: You type a short description, add an optional tag (like "Meeting" or "Focus"), and save it.
  4. Analyze Later: Over time, your Google Sheet becomes a powerful, detailed dashboard of how you spend your time.

That's it! No complex project management, no teams, just a simple log for you.
